## CLIP 1 — The Reveal (0-8s)

A pair of hands holds a matte black shipping mailer. Slow pull back reveals a
clean wooden desk surface. Hands tear open the mailer — inside, a stack of
custom embroidered patches wrapped in tissue paper. Close-up on the top patch:
bold logo, tight stitching, vivid colors. Warm natural light, shallow depth of
field, soft bokeh background. UGC aesthetic — NOT polished commercial.

Style: warm tones, natural light, handheld feel, 9:16 vertical.
No text in frame. No logos visible on packaging.

---

## CLIP 2 — Patch On Jacket (0-8s)

Close-up of a black denim jacket laid flat. A hand places a custom embroidered
patch on the left chest. Slow push-in on the patch — stitching detail visible,
colors sharp. Pull back slightly to show the full jacket with patch in place.
Cinematic, shallow depth of field, warm studio light.

Style: product photography aesthetic, warm tones, clean background, 9:16.

---

## CLIP 3 — Tactical/Military (0-8s)

A gloved hand presses a custom PVC morale patch onto a plate carrier vest.
The patch clicks into velcro with a satisfying snap (imply sound through
motion). Close-up on the patch face — 3D raised design, bold colors.
Pull back to show the full vest setup. Moody lighting, desaturated tones,
tactical aesthetic.

Style: dark, cinematic, desaturated, military/tactical feel, 9:16.

---

## CLIP 4 — Varsity/School Spirit (0-8s)

A letterman jacket laid on a gym bleacher. A hand sews a chenille patch onto
the chest — needle going through fabric, thread pulling tight. Pull back to
reveal the full jacket with multiple patches. School colors visible.
Nostalgic, warm, slightly golden hour light feel.

Style: warm nostalgic tones, authentic feel, no studio gloss, 9:16.

---

## CLIP 5 — Hat Patch (Clothing Brand) (0-8s)

A structured dad hat sits on a wooden surface. Close-up on the front panel
where a custom woven patch is centered — fine detail, small text readable.
A hand picks up the hat, turns it, places it on a surface again. Clean,
minimal, brand aesthetic.

Style: clean minimal, soft shadows, neutral tones, product-first, 9:16.
